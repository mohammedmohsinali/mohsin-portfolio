Numin: AI Market Reasoning, Honestly Scored

// the LLM proposes; the deterministic gate disposes

A market-reasoning platform that turns live market data into versioned, evidence-grounded trade theses through a multi-agent debate pipeline. Every plan must pass a pure-Python risk gate the model cannot override; every call is scored in risk-normalized R-multiples and published to a public transparency page, including the losses. Paper trading only, by design.
